Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa and the most populous nation in Africa, with over 230 million people. It is known for its rich cultural diversity, with more than 250 ethnic groups and over 500 languages. The country's capital is Abuja, while Lagos is its largest city and a major economic and cultural hub. Nigeria has Africa's largest economy, driven by industries such as oil and gas, agriculture, telecommunications, entertainment (Nollywood), and technology. It is also famous for its vibrant music scene, including Afrobeats, and its warm hospitality, diverse traditions, and natural landscapes.
